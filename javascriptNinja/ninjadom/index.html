DOM Ninja

This page follows along with Secrets of a JavaScript Ninja: 2nd edition part 4 (chapters 12 - 15).

JavaScript engines usually have more than 1 event loop queue, even though we talk about it like it's just one. To keep things abstract, we'll just say there's one for MacroTasks and at least one for MicroTasks

NOTE: one each loop of the event loop, JS will execute 1 MacroTask and all the present MicroTasks. These are executes in sequence, one at a time, on the main (read: only) thread.
